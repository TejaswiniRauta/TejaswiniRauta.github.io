5 Powerful Ayurvedic Remedies for Common Cold

Ayurveda offers time-tested natural solutions for treating the common cold. Here are 5 effective remedies:

  • Tulsi Tea: Boil tulsi leaves with ginger and drink it warm.
  • Haldi Milk: Warm milk with turmeric before bed soothes sore throat.
  • Steam Inhalation: Add eucalyptus oil for added benefit.
  • Honey and Ginger: Mix both and take a spoonful twice a day.
  • Ajwain Kada: A powerful decoction that clears nasal congestion.

Note: Consult your doctor before trying any remedies if you have other conditions.
